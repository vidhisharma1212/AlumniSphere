With the rise in cyber-attacks, cybersecurity has become a critical field. Alumni working in cybersecurity roles highlight the demand for professionals with expertise in areas such as network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking. Students are encouraged to pursue certifications like CISSP or CEH, which demonstrate commitment to the field. Lisa White
Alumni emphasize the importance of thorough preparation for both technical and behavioral interviews. For technical interviews, practicing coding problems on platforms like LeetCode or HackerRank is essential, as these assessments often test problem-solving abilities, data structures, and algorithms. System design questions require students to demonstrate a high-level understanding of scalable architectures. On the behavioral side, alumni recommend preparing answers using the STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) method, which helps to structure responses in a way that showcases both accomplishments and thought processes. Researching the company and its culture beforehand is also key, as aligning answers with the companys values can leave a lasting positive impression.
